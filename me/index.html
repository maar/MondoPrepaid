Preuzmi dokument - Procedura za realizaciju programa Mondo Prepaid kartice

The Mondo Card is a tailored solution that pays directly to MasterCard and/or Visa card. It is secure, fast, cost-effective and enables you to easily manage transactions worldwide. Utilizing the Mondo Prepaid MasterCard we enable thousands of Web businesses and other companies to affordably deliver compensation to their suppliers and partners.
