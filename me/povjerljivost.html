Mondo Prepaid poštuje vašu privatnost

Your privacy is very important to us. Accordingly, we have developed this Policy in order for you to understand how we collect, use, communicate and disclose and make use of personal information. The following outlines our privacy policy.

Definicija

A term customer represents you and/or anyone ordering, registering for or making use of our product, services and/or our web site.

Prikupljanje i upotreba informacija o korisniku

Your personal details will be collected and processed when you order, purchase, register, subscribe or make use of our product or services.

In addition, when you visit our website or use our digital eWallet application, we also collect website and application usage information (which may include your reading history and any advertisements you may have viewed), and information about your computer or other digital device and your internet connection. This includes your computer&s or digital device&s IP address, the type and version of browser and operating system you use, your internet domain and, if you visited via a link from another website, the URL of the linking page. This information is collected to help us:

  • fulfil your order
  • manage your registration preferences
  • send you renewal notifications and/or service notifications
  • personalise web content and advertising based on your personal characteristics or preferences
  • ensure, as far as is practical, that our websites and applications are compatible with the browsers and operating systems used by most of our visitors
  • conduct customer satisfaction surveys

We also use your personal information for in-house research and analysis. This helps us to:

  • improve our understanding of customer needs
  • create marketing profiles
  • support strategic development
  • To enable us to conduct research we may share your information with third party suppliers employed to help. All results of any research and analysis will be anonymised or aggregated information and will not reveal any personal details of individual customers.

Kolačići (cookies)

A cookie is a small file which is placed on your hard drive when you visit our web site (directly or via our application). We use information from cookies for purposes which may include:

  • identifying returning users, registrants and subscribers
  • eliminating the need for returning users to re-enter their login details
  • enabling you to move more easily through our web site and application
  • tracking your use of our web site to better develop web site and application in accordance with your needs and requirements
  • building up an anonymous profile based on your browsing patterns across our web site and application, which enables us to make content available to you that is more relevant to your needs
  • conduct customer satisfaction surveys

You can delete cookies from your hard drive at any time. Bear in mind, though, that if you delete them, any settings such as your stored username and password will have to be reset when you next log in. You will be able to use our web site and application even if your browser is not set to accept cookies. However, certain features and content may not be available to you and you will have to log in every time you visit.

By visiting our web site, you consent to the use of cookies. You can also choose whether you wish us to collect cookie information for these purposes using the settings in your browser, as further explained at the web site of the www.cookiecentral.com and their Frequently Asked Question.

Sigurnost

Mondo Prepaid does its utmost to protect your privacy and ensure your personal details are not misused, accidentally destroyed, lost or altered within Mondo Prepaid.

Occasionally we share your details with others who are managing data for us (eg. fulfilment bureaux, application and database consultants etc.). They are only permitted to process your data with explicit permission from Mondo Prepaid, enabling them to perform the services required.

Prenos ličnih podataka korisnika izvan Crne Gore

As the internet is a global environment, collecting and processing personal information may involve the transmission of this data internationally, outside of Montenegro, including outside of the European Union. Therefore, by communicating electronically with us you acknowledge and agree to your personal data being processed in this way.

Djeca ispod 13 godina

Mondo Prepaid does not intentionally collect any personal information from individuals under the age of 13.

When necessary, underage users will be told not to submit any personal details and we will make every effort to delete any details of such users where a parent or guardian has informed us that these details have been collected.

Promjene i dopune ove izjave

From time to time, we may make changes to the privacy policy. This may be in relation to changes in the law, best practice or changes in our services.

Any changes will be reflected in this statement, so you should check here from time to time.
